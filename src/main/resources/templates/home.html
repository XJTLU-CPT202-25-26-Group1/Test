How it works

The workflow is intentionally simple and easy to follow.

1. Request

Customers search specialists, choose an open slot, and submit a request.

2. Review

Administrators confirm or reject requests and maintain workflow visibility.

3. Consult

Specialists manage their schedules and complete confirmed consultations.

4. Track

Customers open booking details, track updates, and leave feedback after completion.
